TRUCE

This installer will guide you through adding the plugin to your system. Pick the formats your DAW understands on the next screen — every format is checked by default, and you can drop any you don't need.

Plug-ins land under /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/ when you install for all users, or under ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/ when you install for yourself only. Restart your DAW after install to pick up the new plug-in.
